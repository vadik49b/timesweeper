How to use TimeSweep:

1. Pick your name from the dropdown

2. Click a cell to mark your availability:
→ ✔ → ❓ →

3. Click and drag to fill multiple cells at once

4. Check "Group availability" to see when everyone is free

5. Click Share to send the link to others

6. When the group agrees, click Confirm

Keyboard shortcuts:
F1 / U — Undo
F2 / F — Focus mode (hide group)
S — Share link
Ctrl+Z — Undo
